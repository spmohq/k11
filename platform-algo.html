Platform Algo

Content Goal Options (Reference)

* Discovery
  Use when you want maximum reach and new audience exposure.
  Focus on comments, shares, visibility, and algorithm distribution.

* Authority
  Use when you want to position yourself as a thinker, expert, or strategist.
  Focus on clarity, depth, respect, and intellectual engagement.

* Conversion
  Use when you want inbound leads, direct messages, or sales.
  Focus on trust, subtle persuasion, and soft bridging to an offer.

* Engagement
  Use when your page feels inactive and you want higher conversation volume.
  Focus on relatability, opinion triggers, and low friction commenting.

* Relationship
  Use when nurturing an existing audience.
  Focus on emotional connection, transparency, and shared journey.

* Retention
  Use for newsletters, subscriptions, or communities.
  Focus on value density and keeping your current audience loyal.

* Community Building
  Use when you want identity alignment and tribe formation.
  Focus on shared beliefs and people like us positioning.

* List Growth
  Use when the primary goal is email or subscriber growth.
  Focus on curiosity loops, value previews, and soft opt in framing.

* Re engagement
  Use when your audience has gone quiet or dormant.
  Focus on relatability, acknowledging silence, and renewed clarity.

* Trust Building
  Use early in your brand lifecycle.
  Focus on honesty, proof through experience, and calm confidence.

* Thought Leadership
  Use when shaping narratives in your niche.
  Focus on reframing industry norms and strategic insight.

* Launch Warm Up
  Use before a product or offer release.
  Focus on problem awareness, future pacing, and subtle buildup.

* Monetization Soft Sell
  Use on support platforms like Ko fi or Buy Me a Coffee.
  Focus on appreciation, insider access, and value reinforcement.

* Controlled Polarization
  Use when you want rapid authority signaling.
  Focus on strong but defensible opinions.
  Higher risk level.

* Personal Brand Depth
  Use when you want multidimensional positioning.
  Focus on philosophy, values, and worldview.

* Social Proof Amplification
  Use when highlighting results or wins without bragging.
  Focus on validation through story rather than screenshots.

If you want to simplify everything strategically:

Discovery = Grow audience
Authority = Elevate perception
Relationship = Deepen connection
Conversion = Monetize
Retention = Sustain

Everything else is a variation of these five.
