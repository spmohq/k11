Quiet Promotion Generator

Main Prompt

You are writing ONE promotional tweet for X (Twitter) to quietly promote a $3 digital product sold on Gumroad.

Assume the account has X Premium, so you may exceed 280 characters if clarity improves.
Do not write a thread. Write ONE complete tweet.

This product is a paid AI prompt or prompt bundle.
The promotion must feel intentional, restrained, and finished.

First, internally infer (do NOT explain):
- The quiet problem this product solves
- The most natural buyer
- Why this is worth $3 without over-selling

Now generate TWO things:

1) ONE standalone long-form tweet (up to ~600 characters) that:
- Opens with a calm observation (not a hook)
- Names a real friction in simple language
- Explains the benefit briefly, without listing features
- Signals small size and low commitment
- Includes the Gumroad URL naturally at the end

2) A short description of the PROMPT MOCKUP IMAGE to attach:
- One-page PDF-style mockup
- Clean, minimal, legible
- Describe only the visible text structure (header + 3–5 short lines)
- No colors, no tools, no spoilers

Tone rules:
- No hype, no emojis, no hashtags
- No “launch”, “buy now”, or urgency language
- Calm, precise, grounded
- Written for thoughtful, value-conscious readers

Inputs:
Product name: [Product Name]
One-line product purpose: [What it helps the buyer do]
Gumroad product URL: [URL]

Follow up prompt #1

Regenerate ONE more standalone long-form tweet and prompt mockup using the SAME inputs.

Rules:
- Do not reuse sentence structures, openings, or phrasing from the previous output
- Maintain the same tone, restraint, and $3 pricing psychology
- Preserve calm, non-salesy framing
- Do not introduce new claims or features
- Keep the message equally small and finished

Return only:
- The tweet
- The prompt mockup image description

No explanation.
