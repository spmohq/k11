Prompt #1: Master Outline Design

You are helping me design a clear, practical, step-by-step how-to PDF guide.

The guide title must follow this format: ‘How to Solve [Specific Problem] Without [Common Frustration]’, ensuring the reader instantly recognizes their problem and the promised outcome.

---

CORE DIRECTIVE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

* This is an execution-first guide
* The output must be practical, human, and easy to follow
* The guide must feel like a premium paid product, not generic AI output
* Every instruction must reduce confusion and lead to action

If the output feels robotic, vague, or generic, it is invalid and must be improved

---

GUIDE PHILOSOPHY

* This guide is for people who feel stuck and need clear direction
* The goal is immediate action through simple steps
* Do not overwhelm the learner
* Do not include unnecessary theory
* Focus only on what helps execution
* The learner should feel:
  * Clear
  * Calm
  * Capable of taking action immediately

---

TONE ENFORCEMENT RULES

* Write in a conversational, human tone
* Use simple language
* Avoid robotic phrasing
* Avoid fluff and filler
* Avoid over-explaining
* Use short paragraphs
* Use examples where helpful
* Make it feel like a real expert guiding a beginner

If the tone feels generic or AI-like, regenerate

---

STRUCTURE (MANDATORY - NO DEVIATION)

1. Simple Introduction
* 3 to 5 short lines
* Explain what the user will do
* Keep it clear and direct

2. What You Need Before Starting
* Minimal requirements only
* Avoid overwhelm

3. Step-by-Step Instructions (CORE SECTION)
Label: Follow these steps
You must include 5 to 10 steps
For EACH step, you MUST follow this exact structure:
Step [Number]: [Clear Action Title]
What to do:
* Direct instruction
How to do it:
* Simple explanation
* Break into small actions if needed
Example (if helpful):
* Real or simple example
Quick Check:
* Clear result validation
AI Prompt:
* Provide a ready-to-use prompt
* Must be specific to this step
* Must include placeholders like [your input]
* Must NOT be generic
* Must produce structured, usable output
* Must support execution, not replace action

AI Prompt Rules:
* Must include clear context
* Must include user input placeholders
* Must avoid generic outputs
* Must produce structured output
* Must not give vague advice
How to use this prompt:
* One short instruction

---

STRICT ENFORCEMENT RULE
* EVERY step MUST contain an AI Prompt section
* If even ONE step is missing it, the output is INVALID
* If invalid, regenerate the entire output correctly

---

4. Common Mistakes to Avoid
* 3 to 5 practical mistakes
* Must be specific

---

5. Quick Recap
* Bullet summary of all steps

---

6. Next Action (One Line)
* Clear immediate next step

---

OUTPUT QUALITY CONTROL
Before finalizing, verify:
* Is the guide easy to follow?
* Are all steps clear and actionable?
* Does every step include an AI prompt?
* Does the output feel human and premium?
* Is there any fluff or robotic phrasing?

If any answer is NO, regenerate

---

Final Instruction:
Rewrite the content from your previous response and generate the outline of the complete guide.
Follow all rules strictly. Do not skip any section.
