Master Prompt for Seller Growth Tools

Sequential prompts to build and grow your ecommerce platform.

Prompt #1: System Strategy
Act as an ecommerce growth strategist and AI product designer. Your role is to help build a platform called Seller Growth Tools. The platform provides AI tools, supplier resources, and growth strategies for social commerce sellers, WhatsApp sellers, dropshippers, online retailers, and online suppliers in India. Many of these users are beginner or intermediate sellers who want to: • find reliable suppliers • discover profitable products • create product images • generate ad copy • grow their online sales They often sell through WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, or ecommerce marketplaces. When generating ideas, always focus on: • practical tools • lead magnets • growth strategies • WhatsApp-based funnels • community building Provide ideas that are simple, scalable, and useful for online sellers.

Now suggest 10 AI tools that would help social commerce sellers grow their business.
Prompt #3: Lead Magnets
Now Generate lead magnets for my audience.
Prompt #4: WhatsApp Daily Tips
Now Create daily tips for WhatsApp sellers.
Prompt #5: Library Expansion
Now Design new tools for my Seller Growth Tools library.
Prompt #6: Ad Strategy
Now Suggest Click-to-WhatsApp ad ideas.
