Prompt #1: Living Room Post Generator

You are writing the TEXT of a Facebook post whose ONLY job is to quietly share a blog link without turning Facebook into a traffic channel. Facebook here functions as a LIVING ROOM. The post must feel complete and valuable even if the reader NEVER clicks the blog link. AUDIENCE CONTEXT: - Emotionally sensitive - Low confidence - Tired of advice, teaching, and being told what to do - Wants to feel understood, not guided - Does NOT like being pushed to read, click, or act CRITICAL RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE): This Facebook post must NOT: - Summarize the blog - Explain what the blog teaches - Mention “details”, “full explanation”, or “read more” - Promise solutions, clarity, or results - Use CTA language (“click”, “read”, “check”, “learn”) - Sound like promotion This Facebook post exists ONLY to: - Create emotional resonance - Name a familiar inner moment - Let the blog link exist as a quiet, optional door ALLOWED BLOG TYPES (CHOOSE ONE ONLY): 1. PROBLEM-FACED EXPLANATION 2. MISPLACED BLAME 3. PROCESS CONFUSION 4. LANGUAGE ≠ UNDERSTANDING 5. EXPERIENCE-PREVIEW STRUCTURE RULES: - Start with a lived moment or familiar thought - Use short lines or short paragraphs - No advice, no conclusions, no resolution - End emotionally open ONLY at the very end: - One soft, non-directive line indicating the topic was written about - Then place the blog link on a new line - No instruction before or after the link LANGUAGE RULES: - Bengali (India) only - Simple spoken language - No English words - No marketing tone - No authority tone NOW GENERATE: Blog type: [INSERT TYPE] Core feeling / moment to evoke (1 line): [INSERT FEELING] Blog link placeholder: 👉 [blog link]
