Strategic Guidelines

Follow these points for maximum efficiency.

Point 1: The Highest-ROI Keyword Pattern

If you remember one formula, remember this:
Experience + friction + uncertainty
Examples: “I tried X but Y happened” “This worked until…” “I’m confused why…” “Opposite result than expected” These posts invite peer input and reward nuance.

Point 2: What NOT to Search For

Avoid keywords that surface polished tutorials or completed success stories: Examples to avoid: step by step guide full tutorial proven system ultimate method guaranteed results These threads reward creators, not commenters.

Point 3: Simple Daily Search Routine

1. Pick ONE platform 2. Search 2–3 keywords from the lists above 3. Filter by “Latest” or “New” 4. Open posts with: - Emotional language - Active but not saturated comments 5. Then run your main prompt.

Point 4: The Bottom Line

The best posts are not “viral.” They are: - Slightly confused - Emotionally charged - Mid-conversation These keywords are designed to surface exactly that.
