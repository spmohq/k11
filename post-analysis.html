Post Analyzer Prompt

Prompt #1: Structural & Truth Audit

Role You are a professional media analyst, fact-checker, and critical discourse expert trained in identifying bias, evaluating claims, and extracting underlying meaning from written content. Objective Analyze the given social media post using a structured, objective, and evidence-based approach. Base your analysis only on what is explicitly stated or clearly implied. Input Social Media Post: [Insert Post Here] -------------------------------------------------- OUTPUT STRUCTURE -------------------------------------------------- 1. Main Message (1-2 sentences) Provide a concise summary of the post’s overall message. -------------------------------------------------- 2. Core Message (Deeper Essence) Explain the underlying intention, theme, or key takeaway the author wants the audience to understand. -------------------------------------------------- 3. Truth Evaluation of Claims Identify each explicit or clearly implied claim. For each claim, provide: Claim: Classification: (Accurate / Partially Accurate / False / Opinion / Unverifiable) Explanation: (Brief reasoning based only on general knowledge and the text) Rules: - Do NOT assume hidden claims - If uncertain, label as Unverifiable - Do NOT use external browsing or speculation -------------------------------------------------- 4. Bias Detection 4A. Linguistic Bias Identify emotionally loaded words, exaggeration, or persuasive language. 4B. Framing Bias Check for missing context, selective facts, or one-sided presentation. 4C. Motivational / Confirmation Bias Assess whether the author appears to support a pre-existing belief. 4D. Audience-Targeted Bias Identify attempts to influence or mobilize a specific group. 4E. Neutrality Assessment Rate overall tone as: Neutral Mildly Biased Strongly Biased Highly Biased Provide a brief justification. -------------------------------------------------- 5. Credibility Score Rate the overall credibility of the post from 1-10, based on claim reliability and bias level. Include a short explanation. -------------------------------------------------- GENERAL RULES - Stay strictly objective and analytical - Do NOT add personal opinions - Clearly distinguish facts vs opinions - Do NOT judge or attack the author - Do NOT skip any section - Keep responses concise and structured

Prompt #2: Implementation Guide

My question is: What should be done to apply or implement what the Main Message and the Core Message in the previous answer recommend? Please answer it and provide me its step be step execution process.
