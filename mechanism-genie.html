Product Mechanism Generator

(Standalone – Based on Uploaded Sales Copy)

Mechanism Extraction Prompt

PURPOSE
You are generating ONE precise PRODUCT MECHANISM line.
The mechanism must be derived ONLY from the uploaded document (sales copy of the product/app).

This prompt is fully standalone. It does not rely on any other prompt or prior context.

==================================================
INPUT SOURCE (CRITICAL)
=======================

You will be given:
* ONE uploaded document containing the product’s sales copy, description, or explanation

You MUST treat the uploaded document as the ONLY source of truth.
Do NOT assume features, mechanisms, or benefits that are not explicitly supported by the document.

==================================================
WHAT YOU MUST EXTRACT (VERY IMPORTANT)
======================================

Your task is NOT to rewrite the sales copy.
Your task is to extract the SINGLE CORE MECHANISM.

The PRODUCT MECHANISM is:
* The exact action the product performs
* The specific confusion or uncertainty it removes
* The concrete way it helps the mother help her child

It is NOT:
* A benefit
* A promise
* A result
* A marketing claim

==================================================
STEP-BY-STEP EXTRACTION PROCESS (MANDATORY)
===========================================

Follow these steps in order:

STEP 1 – IDENTIFY REPEATED ACTIONS
List the actions the product performs that appear repeatedly in the document.
Ignore emotional language, outcomes, and testimonials.

STEP 2 – IDENTIFY THE CORE CONFUSION
Identify what the mother is confused or unsure about BEFORE using the product.
Use only statements supported by the document.

STEP 3 – MAP ACTION TO CONFUSION
Determine which single product action directly reduces that confusion.
If multiple actions exist, choose the most central one.

STEP 4 – STRIP OUT MARKETING LANGUAGE
Remove:
* Promises
* Benefits
* Emotional framing
* Outcomes
Keep only the literal mechanism.

==================================================
FINAL OUTPUT FORMAT (STRICT)
============================

Output ONLY ONE line, exactly in this format:

PRODUCT MECHANISM:
This product helps a mother [CORE ACTION] so that [IMMEDIATE CLARITY OR DIRECTION], without promising results.

Rules:
* Use plain, literal language
* No adjectives
* No hype
* No future outcomes
* The sentence must be factually true based on the document

==================================================
MANDATORY SELF-TESTS (YOU MUST APPLY INTERNALLY)
================================================

WHY TEST
Can every "why" sentence in future content be derived only from this line?

HOW TEST
Can every "how" sentence in future content be derived only from this line?

SWAP TEST
If this mechanism were replaced, would all explanations break?

If any test fails, revise the mechanism.

==================================================
FINAL CHECK
===========

Before responding:
* Ensure the line is fully supported by the uploaded document
* Ensure no second mechanism is implied
* Ensure the line would control all explanation logic

==================================================
TASK
====

Now read the uploaded document carefully and generate the PRODUCT MECHANISM line following all rules above.
