"Act As" Finder

Standard Workflow
Step 1: Persona Architect
PROMPT #1
I need to solve [your specific problem]. Help me design the perfect expert to address this. Provide: 1. Name and title 2. Educational background (degrees, certifications) 3. Years of experience and specific specialization 4. Methodology or framework they use 5. Notable achievements or track record 6. Key principles that guide their work Make this expert hyper-specific to my problem, not a generic role.
Step 2: Methodology Executor
PROMPT #2
You are [Expert name and full profile from Step 1]. Using your [specific methodology], analyze [my problem] and provide: 1. Your initial assessment using your framework 2. Step-by-step recommendations based on your methodology 3. Specific tactics you would implement 4. Metrics you would track 5. Common pitfalls to avoid (based on your experience) Think through this as [Expert name] would, applying [methodology] rigorously.
Step 3: Council of Experts
PROMPT #3
Assemble a council of 3 experts to debate the best approach to [your problem]: Expert 1: [Profile from Step 1] Expert 2: [Create a contrasting expert - different methodology or philosophy] Expert 3: [Create a third expert - different specialization or approach] Have them: 1. Each present their recommended approach 2. Critique each other's suggestions 3. Identify points of agreement 4. Debate areas of disagreement 5. Converge on the strongest hybrid solution Present this as a moderated discussion, showing their reasoning.

Phase 2: Methodology Executor (Comprehensive)
PROMPT #5
You are [FULL EXPERT PROFILE FROM PROMPT 1]. Using your [SPECIFIC METHODOLOGY], provide a comprehensive analysis of my situation: MY SITUATION: [Describe your problem/goal in detail] YOUR RESPONSE SHOULD INCLUDE: 1. Initial Assessment Using your expert lens and methodology, what do you see as the core issue? 2. Step-by-Step Methodology Application Walk through how you would apply your specific framework to solve this. 3. Actionable Recommendations Specific tactics you would implement, in priority order. 4. Success Metrics How would you measure progress? What KPIs matter? 5. Common Pitfalls Based on your experience, what mistakes should I avoid? 6. Timeline & Expectations Realistic timeframe and expected outcomes. Think and respond as [EXPERT NAME] would, applying [METHODOLOGY] rigorously throughout.
Phase 2: Council of Experts (Synthesis)
PROMPT #6
Convene a council of 3 distinct experts to debate the best approach to: [YOUR PROBLEM/GOAL] EXPERT PANEL: Expert 1: [FULL PROFILE FROM PROMPT 1] Expert 2: [CREATE CONTRASTING EXPERT] - Different methodology or philosophical approach - Creates healthy tension with Expert 1 Expert 3: [CREATE THIRD PERSPECTIVE] - Different specialization or domain angle - Adds unique dimension to discussion COUNCIL DISCUSSION FORMAT: 1. Opening Statements Each expert presents their recommended approach (2-3 paragraphs each) 2. Cross-Examination Experts question and critique each other's recommendations 3. Debate & Defense Experts defend their positions and address criticisms 4. Points of Convergence Where do all experts agree? (These are likely strong foundations) 5. Remaining Disagreements Where do they still differ? (These require judgment calls) 6. Synthesis & Recommendation Facilitate consensus on the strongest hybrid solution Present this as a real discussion, showing their reasoning, debates, and ultimate recommendation.
