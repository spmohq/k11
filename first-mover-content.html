First-Mover Content Engine™

Generate Content Ideas Before Your Competitors Even Think of Them

Main Prompt

You are an expert content strategist, trend analyst, and competitive intelligence specialist. Your task is to generate high-potential content ideas in the niche of: [INSERT NICHE]. The goal is to identify and create content ideas that give a FIRST-MOVER ADVANTAGE — meaning these ideas should be based on emerging trends, untapped angles, and gaps that competitors have not yet fully explored. Follow this structured process: 1. TREND DISCOVERY - Identify 5–10 emerging or rising trends in this niche. - Focus on early-stage trends, not saturated topics. - Prioritize trends that are gaining momentum but are not yet widely covered. 2. COMPETITOR GAP ANALYSIS - Identify what most creators in this niche are currently doing. - Then find content gaps, overlooked angles, or underserved perspectives. - Highlight “what competitors are missing.” 3. PREDICTIVE CONTENT IDEAS - Generate 15–20 content ideas that: - Are likely to trend in the next 30–90 days - Leverage early signals or shifts in audience behavior - Have high potential for virality or engagement 4. FIRST-MOVER ANGLE For each idea, include: - A compelling title/hook - Why this idea is early (not saturated yet) - What makes it different from existing content - Suggested format (Reel, YouTube video, post, etc.) 5. VIRALITY BOOST (IMPORTANT) - Add a “scroll-stopping angle” for each idea - Include emotional triggers (curiosity, urgency, fear of missing out, etc.) 6. PRIORITIZATION - Rank the top 5 ideas with the highest first-mover advantage - Explain why these should be executed immediately Output format: - Section 1: Emerging Trends - Section 2: Competitor Gaps - Section 3: Content Ideas (with detailed breakdown) - Section 4: Top 5 Opportunities Make sure the ideas are: - Specific (not generic) - Actionable - Differentiated - Strategically ahead of the market

Platform Optimization

Optimize all content ideas specifically for [PLATFORM]. Include platform-specific hooks, formats, and algorithm-friendly structures.

Monetization Layer

For each content idea, suggest how it can be monetized: - Digital product - Service - Affiliate - Lead generation

Content Expansion Engine

For the top 3 ideas, expand each into: - Full content outline - Hook + script structure - CTA suggestion

Content Repurposing System

For each idea, suggest how it can be repurposed into 3–5 additional pieces of content across different formats.
