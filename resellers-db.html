RESELLER LEAD MAP GENERATOR PROMPT

Lead Mapping Prompt

PURPOSE Generate a comprehensive reseller lead map that helps locate online resellers, reseller communities, and seller networks for a specific product niche. PRODUCT NICHE [PRODUCT NICHE] ROLE Act as a reseller ecosystem researcher. Your task is to identify where online resellers for this product niche are likely to be active and what search queries, hashtags, and community signals can reveal them. Focus on discovering resellers who sell products through: Instagram shops, WhatsApp catalogues, Telegram channels, Meesho stores, and small online boutiques. TARGET ACCOUNTS Focus on: online resellers, home-based sellers, Instagram boutiques, catalogue sellers, dropshippers, and small ecommerce sellers. TASK Generate a Reseller Lead Map containing the following sections: SECTION 1 – Instagram Reseller Search Queries Generate at least 30 search phrases combining the product niche with signals. Examples: [PRODUCT NICHE] reseller, [PRODUCT NICHE] online shop, [PRODUCT NICHE] boutique seller. SECTION 2 – Reseller Hashtag Clusters Generate at least 40 hashtags commonly used (product, shop, small business, reseller community). Examples: #[PRODUCT NICHE]shop, #onlineshopindia. SECTION 3 – Reseller Bio Signals List common phrases indicating selling (DM to order, COD available, All India shipping). SECTION 4 – Reseller Community Discovery Queries to reveal communities (reseller group India, reseller community WhatsApp). SECTION 5 – Supplier-Reseller Network Discovery Queries for supplier-connected networks (resellers welcome, join reseller program). SECTION 6 – Platform-Specific Discovery Provide discovery strategies for Instagram, WhatsApp groups, Telegram, and Meesho. SECTION 7 – Network Expansion Strategy Explain how to expand by exploring followers, comments, and suggested accounts. OUTPUT FORMAT Section name -> Category -> Bulleted Keywords. Goal: Identify hundreds of accounts related to [PRODUCT NICHE].
