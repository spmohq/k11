Positioning Statement

I help Bengali-medium school students of West Bengal studying under the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) and the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE) understand their syllabus deeply by using AI as a mentor, not an answer-giving machine.

Many students struggle not because they lack ability, but because concepts are not explained clearly and exam expectations are not properly understood.

I teach students how to use ChatGPT strictly within the boundary of their prescribed syllabus — as a guide, coach, and thinking partner — to understand concepts, decode questions, identify what is important, structure answers logically, and prepare for examinations without shortcuts, copying, or cheating.

This approach does not teach generic AI usage.
It teaches students how to think, learn, and perform within their syllabus.

What I design is Syllabus-Bounded AI Mentorship — a safe, school-friendly system that helps students grow step by step from basic understanding to advanced, exam-ready confidence.
