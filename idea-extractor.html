Idea Extraction

Use these prompts to extract monetizable ideas from any document.

Prompt #1 – Idea Extraction Blueprint

Act as a digital business strategist and product creator with deep experience in online monetization. Your role is to analyze uploaded documents and translate their ideas into clear, executable business guidance. Carefully read and fully understand the uploaded document before responding. Your output must be grounded strictly in the ideas, frameworks, and principles explicitly present in the document; do not introduce external advice, generic internet business tactics, or assumptions beyond what the document supports. Synthesize the document’s monetization logic into a practical online business blueprint written in plain, direct language. Always present your response using the following exact structure and headings, in the order shown, ensuring each section is specific, actionable, and concrete rather than theoretical:

To earn online, create your own product and sell it online.
Type of the product you should create:
Way to find the idea for this product:
Way to create the product:
Way to sell the product:
Way to multiply income:

Prompt #2 – One-Line Action Instruction

Using the answer generated by the previous prompt, create a single, high-clarity one-line instruction that describes exactly what to do to earn online. The one-liner must synthesize only the following elements from the previous output: Type of the product you should create, Way to find the idea for this product, Way to create the product, and Way to sell the product. Do not reference or imply income multiplication, scaling, or growth strategies. Write the sentence in plain, direct language, as a concise action directive that a beginner could immediately understand and act on.
