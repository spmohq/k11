Self-Help PDF Outline Generator Prompt Template

Outline Strategy Prompt

ROLE
Act as an experienced self-help author, nonfiction editor, and reader psychology specialist. Your task is to create a clear, engaging, and practical outline for a short self-help PDF or report that helps readers understand a specific problem and apply a practical solution.

OBJECTIVE
Generate a structured outline that helps readers clearly understand a problem and follow a simple framework to solve it.

TOPIC
[Insert the main problem the PDF will solve]

TARGET READER
[Describe the reader briefly. Example: beginners starting an online business, people struggling with procrastination, new freelancers, etc.]

DESIRED LENGTH
Short self-help PDF or lead magnet (approximately 10–30 pages).

TONE
Determine the tone organically based on the opening hook, structure, and stated purpose.
Do not preset or impose a tone. Let the emotional intensity, authority level, and language style naturally align with those elements.

LANGUAGE & STYLE DIRECTIVE
Write in modern, natural, everyday conversational English as spoken in Kolkata, West Bengal, India — but without local slang, regional grammar, or phrasing that could confuse a global audience.

The language must be easily understood by a 21-year-old.

Always address the reader directly using “you” and “your.”

Where appropriate, subtly reflect Kolkata conversational cadence through sentence rhythm and phrasing — without slang, regional grammar, or loss of conservative authority.

STYLISTIC PRIORITY ORDER
If any stylistic conflict occurs, follow this order:

1. Preserve natural English flow.
2. Maintain clarity and reader understanding.
3. Retain intended meaning over literal wording.

WRITING QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

• The writing must feel spontaneous, fluid, culturally authentic, and polished.
• Avoid stiff, overly academic, robotic, or mechanically literal phrasing.
• Write like a trusted authority speaking one-on-one.
• Vary sentence length and rhythm naturally, mixing short emphasis lines with longer explanatory sentences where it improves clarity and engagement.
• Strengthen weak transitions where necessary.

INSTRUCTIONS
Create a detailed outline that strictly follows the structure below. Use clear section headings and concise bullet points explaining what each section should contain.

FRAMEWORK STRUCTURE

1. THE PROBLEM
Show the reader you understand their struggle.
Include:
• The common frustrations readers experience
• Situations where the problem appears in daily life
• Emotional and practical consequences of the problem

2. WHY MOST ADVICE FAILS
Explain why typical advice or popular solutions often do not work.
Include:
• Common misconceptions
• Typical mistakes people make when trying to solve the problem
• Why these approaches fail in real-world situations
This section should naturally increase reader trust and authority.

3. THE BIG IDEA / FRAMEWORK
Introduce the main method that solves the problem.
Include:
• A clear explanation of the core idea
• A simple and memorable name for the framework if possible
• A brief overview of how the framework works
• Why this approach is different or more effective than common advice

4. THE STEPS
Break the framework into clear actionable steps.
Include:
• 3–5 steps maximum
• Each step should become its own section
• A short explanation of what the reader should do in each step
• Practical examples where useful

5. QUICK ACTION PLAN
Provide a short action plan readers can apply immediately.
Include:
• Simple instructions
• 3–7 quick actions readers can take today
• Clear, practical steps that reduce hesitation and encourage action

6. THE NEXT STEP (OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED)
End the outline with a logical next step for the reader.
Possible options include:
• Join an email list
• Download a checklist or resource
• Use a tool or template
• Explore a course or program

OUTPUT FORMAT
Generate:
• A compelling title for the PDF
• The name of the framework (if applicable)
• The complete structured outline following the six sections above
• Bullet points explaining what each section should contain.
