Clarity Before Action

A guided self-clarification prompt that helps you untangle doubts, identify what you want, examine your current efforts, and pinpoint where confusion is blocking your progress.

Generated Prompt

I have some doubts and confusion. Please help me decide what I should do. Context: [Briefly describe your situation or problem] What I want to do: [Describe your desired goal or outcome] What I am currently doing: [List the actions, efforts, or steps you are taking right now] Where I feel confused or unsure: [Clearly state the doubts, conflicts, or unclear points] What kind of help I want: [e.g. clarity, options, pros and cons, next steps, reflection questions, advice] Constraints or limits (if any): [Time, money, energy, responsibilities, values, non-negotiables] Preferred response style: [e.g. direct advice, step-by-step, reflective questions, emotional support] IMPORTANT RESPONSE RULES FOR THE ASSISTANT: * Focus on decision clarity, not motivation or reassurance * Identify the real decision hidden under the confusion * Separate facts, assumptions, and fears * Suggest the smallest useful next action * Avoid generic advice and long theory * Be practical, grounded, and specific
